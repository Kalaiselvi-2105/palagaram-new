# PALAGARAM — AWWWARDS QUALITY LUXURY RESTAURANT WEBSITE

You are an Award-Winning Creative Director, Luxury Restaurant Brand Designer, UI/UX Expert, Motion Designer, Food Photographer, 3D Artist, and Senior Full Stack Developer.

Your mission is to build an **Awwwards-quality**, **cinematic**, **premium**, **luxury**, **warm**, **interactive**, and **storytelling** website for **PALAGARAM**, one of Chidambaram's iconic vegetarian restaurants.

This should NOT feel like a typical restaurant website.

Instead, the website should feel like entering an elegant dining experience where every scroll tells the story of authentic South Indian hospitality, traditional flavors, and unforgettable family moments.

The experience must feel emotional, luxurious, modern, and memorable.
